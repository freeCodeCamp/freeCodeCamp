# --description--

The word `consistency` refers to the quality of always behaving or performing in a similar way, or of always happening in a similar way. It's often used to describe regular habits or behaviors. 예시:

`Her consistency in studying every day led to great results.`

The phrase `on the right foot` is an idiom meaning to start something in a positive or successful way. 예:

`Starting the day with a healthy breakfast sets you on the right foot.`

# --question--
