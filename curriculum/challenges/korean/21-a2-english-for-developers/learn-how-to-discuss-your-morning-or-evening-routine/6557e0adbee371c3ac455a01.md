# --description--

You probably remember that `I'll` is the contracted form of `I will`, a modal verb used to express future intentions, decisions, or plans made at the moment of speaking. 예시:

`I'll call you tomorrow.` (future intention to call) `I'll take the bus to work.` (decision made about taking the bus)
