# --description--

The imperative form is used to give orders, instructions, or advice. It often starts with a verb in the base form for positive commands, or with `don't` for negative commands. Understanding this form is important for following or giving instructions. 예시:

`Drink plenty of water` (positive command)

`Don't stay up too late` (negative command)
