# --explanation--

`Me gusta` means "I like," while `te gusta` means "you like."

The verb `gustar` is always used with indirect object pronouns (`me`, `te`, `le`, etc.). For example:

- `Me gusta leer.` – I like to read.

- `¿Te gusta el café?` – Do you like coffee?
