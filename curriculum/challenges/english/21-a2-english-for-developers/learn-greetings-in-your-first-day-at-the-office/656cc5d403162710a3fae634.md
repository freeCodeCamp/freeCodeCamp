# --description--

If a person asks you a direct question using `is it`. For example: `Is it true?` You can reply to this question with these short answers:

`Yes, it is.` For affirmative. 
`No, it is not` or `No, it isn't`. For negative.

# --questions--

## --text--

## --answers--
