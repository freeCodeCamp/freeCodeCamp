# --explanation--

The word `soy` comes from the verb `ser` and is used to **express identity, origin, or profession**.

In this case, `Soy ingeniero de software` means **I am a software engineer.**

Use `soy` when describing **who you are** or **what you do**. For example:

- `Soy diseñador UX.` – I am a UX designer. (masculine)

- `Soy profesora.` – I am a teacher. (feminine)
