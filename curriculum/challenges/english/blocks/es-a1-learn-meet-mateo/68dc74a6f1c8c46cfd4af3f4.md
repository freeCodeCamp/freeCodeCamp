# --description--

Mateo is using a common greeting used from **noon until sunset**.  
There is a different article used for `días` vs `noches`, due to the gender of the noun:  
`días` ("days") is **masculine**, while `noches` ("nights") is **feminine**.  

| Phrase | Meaning | Example |
|---------|----------|----------|
| `Buenos días` | Good morning | `Buenos días, ¿cómo está usted?` (Good morning, how are you?) |
| `Buenas noches` | Good evening/night | `Buenas noches, permítame presentarme`. (Good evening, allow me to introduce myself.) |
