# --explanation--

The phrase `me llamo` comes from the reflexive verb `llamarse`, which means **to call oneself**. In Spanish, when introducing yourself, you say **"I call myself [name]"** instead of **"My name is [name]"**. For example:

- `Me llamo Mateo.` - My name is Mateo.  
- `Me llamo Julieta.` - My name is Julieta.  
- `¿Cómo te llamas?` - What’s your name?
