# --explanation--

The phrase `Soy científica de datos` means "I am a data scientist."  

The verb `soy` comes from `ser`, and is used to describe profession, nationality, or permanent characteristics. For example:

`Soy programador.` – I am a programmer.

In Spanish, many professions change their ending depending on whether the speaker is **female** or **male**. For example:

- `Científica` – Scientist. (feminine)

- `Científico` – Scientist. (masculine)
