# --explanation--

The phrase `Tengo 28 años` means **“I am 28 years old.”**

In Spanish, the verb `tener` (to have) is used to talk about **age**. For example:

- `Tengo 28 años.` - I have 28 years. (literally) - I am 28 years old.

- `Tengo 15 años.` – I am 15 years old.  

- `Tengo 40 años.` – I am 40 years old.

The word `años` means *years*. Notice the special character `ñ`.
