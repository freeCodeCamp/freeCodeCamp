# --description--

Julieta is mentioning her profession in Spanish.

The verb `ser` is used to express the **identity**, **profession**, and **nationality** of the speaker. As we have seen in previous lessons, the structure is:

1. `Soy` ("I am") + name.

2. `Soy` ("I am") + profession.

3. `Soy` ("I am") + nationality.
