# --explanation--

`你好(nǐ hǎo)` is a common greeting in Chinese, which means hello. Here're examples:

- `你好(nǐ hǎo)，王先生(wáng xiān shēng)` - Hello, Mr. Wang.

- `陈女士(chén nǚ shì)，你好(nǐ hǎo) ` - Hello, MS. Chen.
