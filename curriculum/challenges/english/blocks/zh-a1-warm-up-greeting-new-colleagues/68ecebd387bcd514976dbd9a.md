# --explanation--

Wang Hua is saying a nationality: `中国人 (zhōng guó rén)`, which means Chinese.

It combines `中国 (zhōng guó)`, which means China, and `人 (rén)`, which means people. For example:

`我是中国人 (wǒ shì zhōng guó rén)` - I am Chinese.
