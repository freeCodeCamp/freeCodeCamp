### --feedback--

# --explanation--

`你们好 (nǐ men hǎo)` is the plural form of greeting in Chinese. You use it when greeting more than one person, such as colleagues or friends.

- greeting classmates: `同学们，你们好 (tóng xué men, nǐ men hǎo)` – Hello, classmates.  
- greeting colleagues: `大家好，你们好 (dà jiā hǎo, nǐ men hǎo)` – Hi everyone.  
- greeting a group of friends: `朋友们，你们好 (péng yǒu men, nǐ men hǎo)` – Hello, friends.
