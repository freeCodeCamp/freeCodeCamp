## Glossary

Review this glossary as a quick reference of the most important words and phrases in the dialogue.

### Greetings & Intro

- `Hola` - Hello.
- `¿Cómo estás?` - How are you?
- `Gracias` - Thank you.
- `¿Y tú?` - And you?

<br>

### Daily Routines & Time

- `Esta mañana` - This morning.
- `Antes del trabajo` - Before work.
- `El día` - The day.
- `Por la mañana` - In the morning.

<br>

### Feelings & Preferences

- `Bien` - Well.
- `Feliz` - Happy.
- `Cansada` / `Cansado` - Tired.
- `Me gusta` - I like.
- `Sí` - Yes.
- `Buena energía` - Good energy.

<br>

### Actions & Verbs

- `Escuché` - I listened.
- `Bailé` - I danced.
- `Bailas` - You dance.
- `Empezar` - To start/ begin.

<br>

### Work & Context

- `Trabajo` - Work / Job.

<br>
