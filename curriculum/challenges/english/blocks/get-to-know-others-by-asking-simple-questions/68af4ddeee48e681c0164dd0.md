# Glossary

Review this glossary as a quick reference of the most important words and phrases in the dialogue.

## Greetings & Basics

- `Hola` - Hello.

- `Claro` - Of course.

- `Por supuesto` - Certainly / Of course.  

<br>

## Verbs & Actions

- `Tener` - To have.

- `Tienes` - You have.

- `Tengo` - I have.

- `Estar` - To be (temporary).

- `Estás` - You are.

- `Estoy` - I am.

- `Gustar` - To like.

- `Te gustan` - You like (plural things).

- `Me gustan` - I like (plural things).

- `No me gustan` - I don’t like.

- `Poder` - Can / To be able.

- `Puedes` - You can.

- `Ayudar` - To help.

- `Ayudarme` - To help me.

- `Entiendo` - I understand.

- `Nos vemos` - See you.  

<br>

## Nouns

- `Minuto` - Minute.

- `Reunión` - Meeting.

- `Datos` - Data.

- `Experiencia` - Experience.
