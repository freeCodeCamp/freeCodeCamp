# --description--

In this task, you will practice recognizing and understanding numbers in the 70s in Spanish.

Numbers from 31 to 99 (except 21-29) usually follow this pattern:

tens + `y` + number

For example:

- `Setenta y cuatro` - 74

- `Setenta y cinco` - 75

- `Setenta y nueve` - 79
