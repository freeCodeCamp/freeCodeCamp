# --description--

In this task, you will practice recognizing how to ask and answer questions about work.

The question begins with `¿Dónde?`, which is an interrogative word used to ask about **location** (where). For example:

- `¿Dónde vives?` (Where do you live?)

- `¿Dónde está el baño?` (Where is the bathroom?)

When Elena answers, she talking in **first person** because she is talking about herself NOT about the company.
