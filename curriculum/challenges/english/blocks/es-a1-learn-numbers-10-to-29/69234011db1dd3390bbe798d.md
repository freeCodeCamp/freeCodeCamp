# --description--

The numbers 21, 22, and 23 follow a similar pattern.

The number 21 is called `Veintiuno`. This number is unique because when it's paired with a masculine noun like `años`, it changes to `Veintiún`, with an accent mark in the letter `u`.

The number 22 is called `Veintidós`, with an accent mark in the letter `o`.

The number 23 is called `Veintitrés`, with an accent mark in the letter `e`.

These numbers are a combination of `Veinti` and the corresponding number (`uno` for 1, and `dos` for 2, and `tres` for 3).

Listen to their pronunciations and practice repeating them until you feel more comfortable with their sounds.
