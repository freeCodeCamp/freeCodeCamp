## `Veintiuno` vs. `Veintiún`

`Veintiuno` is the full form of the number 21. It's used when this word acts as a standalone noun or when referring to a number without directly modifying another noun. For example:

`El resultado es veintiuno` - The result is twenty-one.

In contrast, `veintiún` is used when the word precedes and modifies a masculine noun. For example:

`Tengo veintiún años` - I'm twenty-one years old.

Note that the word `veintiún` has an accent mark in the letter `u`.
