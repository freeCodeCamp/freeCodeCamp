# --explanation--

The correct answer is `¿De dónde eres?` - `¿A dónde vas?` because this pair uses two different informal questions that ask about two different concepts:

`¿De dónde eres?` means "Where are you from?". Its correct formal equivalent is `¿De dónde es usted?`.

`¿A dónde vas?` means "Where are you going?". Its correct formal equivalent is `¿A dónde va?`.

The other options show the correct transformations from the informal (`tú`) question to the formal (`usted`) question.
