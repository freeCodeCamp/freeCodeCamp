What is the exact value for `sin(105°)`?

## --answers--

`tan(60° + 45°) = (tan(60°) + tan(45°)) / (1 - tan(60°)tan(45°))`

`cos(15°) = cos(60°−45°) = cos(60°)cos(45°) + sin(60°)sin(45°) = (1/2)(√2/2) + (√3/2)(√2/2) = (√6 + √2)/4`
