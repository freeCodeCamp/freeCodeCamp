### --feedback--

# --explanation--

`¿Cuál...?` is used to ask for specific details. For example:

- `¿Cuál es tu correo electrónico?` - What is your email?

`Su` is a possessive adjective used in third person. In this dialogue, it refers to the company. For example:

- `Su oficina está en Lima.` - His/her/its office is in Lima.

`Es` is third person singular form of the verb `ser`. For example:

- `El sitio web es oficial.` - The website is official.

- `La dirección es correcta.` - The address is correct.
