# --description--

In this task, Sebastián is talking about a company. Since a company is a singular noun, we use the third person singular form of the verb.

The verb `es` comes from `ser` (to be).

`Es` is used with `él` / `ella` / `usted`, and also with singular nouns like `la empresa`, `el equipo`, or a company name.

Whenever we describe what a company or singular noun is, we use `es` because the subject is third person singular. For example:

- `Google es una empresa internacional`. - Google is an international company.

- `La oficina es grande`. - The office is big.

- `El proyecto es importante`.  - The project is important.
