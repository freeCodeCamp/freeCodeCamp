# --description--

For any $N$, let $f(N)$ be the last five digits before the trailing zeroes in $N!$.

For example,

$$\begin{align}
  & 9! = 362880 \\; \text{so} \\; f(9) = 36288 \\\\
  & 10! = 3628800 \\; \text{so} \\; f(10) = 36288 \\\\
  & 20! = 2432902008176640000 \\; \text{so} \\; f(20) = 17664
\end{align}$$

Find $f(1,000,000,000,000)$
