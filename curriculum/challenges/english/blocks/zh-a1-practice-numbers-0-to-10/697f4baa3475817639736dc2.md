Wang Hua is saying `四是四 (sì shì sì)，十是十 (shí shì shí) `, which means "4 is 4, 10 is 10". This is a common Chinese tongue twister to help you practice the pronunciation of similar-sounding numbers `四 (sì)`, `十 (shí)` with the character `是 (shì)`.
