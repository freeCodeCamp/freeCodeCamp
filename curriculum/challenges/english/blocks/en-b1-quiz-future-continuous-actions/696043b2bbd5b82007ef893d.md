# --description--

This quiz checks your understanding of how to talk about future intentions, expectations, and workplace planning.

To pass the quiz, you must correctly answer at least 18 of the 20 questions below.

Read each question and choose the correct answer. There's only one correct answer for each question.

For accessibility, transcripts are available for learners with hearing impairments; we recommend using them only if needed.

#### --distractors--
