In this task, you will hear Ángela ask someone for some personal details. The question uses `¿cuál?`, which is commonly used in Spanish to ask for specific information or to identify something among options, especially when followed by the verb `ser` (to be). For example:

# --explanation--

`¿Tu nombre?` – (literally: "Your name?") is short for `¿Cuál es tu nombre?`
