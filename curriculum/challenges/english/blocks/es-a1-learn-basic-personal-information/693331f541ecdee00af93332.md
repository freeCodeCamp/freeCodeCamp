# --explanation--

Spanish often uses the words **`cuál`** and **`cómo`** to form common questions. **`¿Cuál ... ?`** is used to ask for specific information, especially before the verb **`ser`**. For example:

- `¿Cuál es tu nombre?` – What is your name?

- `¿Cuál es tu número de teléfono?` – What is your phone number?

**`¿Cómo ... ?`** is used to ask **how** something is done, spelled, or pronounced. For example:

- `¿Cómo te llamas?` – What’s your name?

- `¿Cómo se escribe?` – How is it spelled?
