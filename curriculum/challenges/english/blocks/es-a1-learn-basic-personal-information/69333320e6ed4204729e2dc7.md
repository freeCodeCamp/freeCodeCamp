### --feedback--

This is the correct form of the verb `ser`, used to say "I am."

# --explanation--

`Soy` means "I am" and comes from the verb `ser`. It's used to describe permanent characteristics like nationality, identity, and profession. For example:

- `Soy de Perú.` – I am from Peru.

- `Soy peruana.` – I am Peruvian. (feminine)

- `Soy peruano.` – I am Peruvian. (masculine)
