# --description--

`Le` is a pronoun that represents "to him", "to her", or "to you".

`Le gusta` means "he likes" when referring to Diego, who is a male. This is the same when referring to a female. For example:

- `Le gusta el fútbol` - He likes football (referring to a male).

- `Le gusta el fútbol` - She likes football (referring to a female).
