# --description--

To say what someone likes in Spanish, you can use this structure:

**`Le gusta` ("He/She likes") + What the person likes.**

The pronoun `le` refers to "him" or "her". It makes it clear that you are talking about another person. 

To say that someone **doesn't** like something, you would add the word `no` before `le`. 

**`No le gusta` ("He/She doesn't like") + What the person doesn't like.**

Sebastián is talking about what Marisol likes to do in her daily work.
