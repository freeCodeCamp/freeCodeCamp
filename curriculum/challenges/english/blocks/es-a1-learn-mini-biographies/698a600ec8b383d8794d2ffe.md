# --description-- 

To talk about someone's residence, you can use the verb `vivir` ("to live") followed by the preposition `en` ("in") and the current location. For example:

- `Él vive en Montevideo, Uruguay.` - He lives in Montevideo, Uruguay.

- `Ella vive en Buenos Aires, Argentina` - She lives in Buenos Aires, Argentina.
