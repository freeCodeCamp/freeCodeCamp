# --explanation--

To talk about where someone is currently living, you can use the verb `vivir` ("to live"). 

For a third person (she or he), the conjugated verb is `vive` ("lives"). For example:

`Ella vive en Lima.` - She lives in Lima.

It's common to omit the `ella` or `él` if the context is clear enough, like this:

`Vive en Lima.` - She lives in Lima.
