# --explanation--

`Le gusta` means "He/She likes".

`Organizar` means "to organize".

`Productos` means "products".

`Y` means "and".

`Equipos` means "teams".

Therefore, when Julieta says this about Valeria: `Le gusta organizar productos y equipos`, she's saying that Valeria likes to organize products and teams.
