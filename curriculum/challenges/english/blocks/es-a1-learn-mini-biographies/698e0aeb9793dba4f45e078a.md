# --explanation--

Sebastián says that Marisol `tiene cuarenta y un años`, which means that she is forty-one years old.

Remember that when the number `uno` ("one") comes before a masculine noun like `años`, it changes to `un`. Therefore, `cuarenta y uno` becomes `cuarenta y un años`.
