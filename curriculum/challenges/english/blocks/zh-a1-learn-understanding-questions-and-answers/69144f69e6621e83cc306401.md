# --explanation--

`是的 (shì de)` means "yes" or "that's right". It's a common way to confirm something or give a positive response. For example:

Speaker A: `你是中国人吗 (nǐ shì zhōng guó rén ma)？` – Are you Chinese?

Speaker B: `是的 (shì de)。` – Yes.
