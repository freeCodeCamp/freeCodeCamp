## --text--

Which phrase means "likewise"?

## --answers--

`你也是 (nǐ yě shì)`

### --feedback--

This means "you too".

---

`我也是 (wǒ yě shì)`

---

`我不是 (wǒ bù shì)`

### --feedback--

This means "I am not".

---

`我是的 (wǒ shì de)`

### --feedback--

This means "I'm".

## --video-solution--

2

# --explanation--

`我 (wǒ)` means "I". `也 (yě)` means "also" or "too". `是 (shì)` means "to be".

Together, `我也是 (wǒ yě shì)` is used to express "likewise" in response to someone else's statement.
