## --text--

How does Wang Hua introduce her name?

## --answers--

`你好 (nǐ hǎo)`

### --feedback--

This is a typical greeting, but it does not include her name.  review the structure she is using to introduce her name.

`我是中国人 (wǒ shì zhōng guó rén)。`

### --feedback--

### --feedback--
