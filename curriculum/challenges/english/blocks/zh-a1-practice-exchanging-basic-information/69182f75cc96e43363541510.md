# --description--

Anna has just started learning Chinese. Today is her first day at her new company. She wants to greet her colleague in simple Chinese and introduce herself politely.

# --instructions--

Find the correct words from the list below to help Anna complete the short conversation.  

**IMPORTANT**: Some words won't be used, others simply don't make sense in context, and you can only use each word **once**.

`请问`, `你吗`, `你好`, `我也是`, `叫什么`, `中国人`, `你呢`

## --blanks--

`你好`

### --feedback--

This is a common greeting meaning "hello".

---

`请问`

### --feedback--

This is used at the beginning of a question to make it more polite.

---

`叫什么`

### --feedback--

This phrase is used when asking for someone's name.

---

`你呢`

### --feedback--

This is used to ask about the same information from the other person.

---

`我也是`

### --feedback--

This means "likewise", used to express that you feel the same way as the other person.

# --explanation--

Anna uses the greeting `你好` to start the conversation. She asks politely using `请问` and asks for his name with `叫什么`. After Liu Ming introduces himself, Anna says her profession and uses `你呢` to ask about his. Then, she responds with `我也是` to express that she is also happy to meet him.
