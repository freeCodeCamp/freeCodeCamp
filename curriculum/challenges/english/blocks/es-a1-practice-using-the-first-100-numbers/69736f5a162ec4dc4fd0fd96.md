# --explanation--

Numbers from 30 to 90 follow a regular pattern. Most of them end in `-enta`:

- 30 = `Treinta`  

- 40 = `Cuarenta` 

- 50 = `Cincuenta` 

- 60 = `Sesenta`  

- 70 = `Setenta`  

- 80 = `Ochenta`  

- 90 = `Noventa`  

The number 100 is different:

100 = `Cien`
