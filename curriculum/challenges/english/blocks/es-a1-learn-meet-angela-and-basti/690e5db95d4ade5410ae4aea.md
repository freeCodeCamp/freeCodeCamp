# --description--

The phrase **`Me llamo` + name** is the most common way to introduce yourself in Spanish.

You can use it in both informal and formal settings to introduce yourself politely.

| Subject | Reflexive Pronoun | Verb Conjugation | Context |
| :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **Yo** ("I") | **Me** | **llamo** | Standard way to say your name. |
| **Tú** ("You") | **Te** | **llamas** | Used in informal settings. |
| **Él/Ella/Usted** ("He"/"She"/"You") | **Se** | **llama** | `Usted` is used in formal settings. |
