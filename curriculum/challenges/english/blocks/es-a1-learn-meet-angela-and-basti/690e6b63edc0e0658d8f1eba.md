# --description--

The phrase **`Me llaman` + name** is commonly used to introduce an `apodo` ("nickname") or a shortened version of a person's name.

| Subject | Object Pronoun | Verb Conjugation | Full Phrase (Structure) |
| :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **`Yo`** ("Me") | `Me` | `llaman` | `Me llaman` [Nickname]. |
| **`Tú`** ("You") | `Te` | `llaman` | `Te llaman` [Nickname]. |
| **`Él/Ella/Usted`** ("He"/"She"/"You") | `Le` / `Lo`/`La` | `llaman` | `Le llaman` [Nickname]. |
