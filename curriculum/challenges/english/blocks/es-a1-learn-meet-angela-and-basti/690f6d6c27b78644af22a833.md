# --description--

The phrase **`Tengo` + [age]+ `años`** is the most common way to say your age in Spanish. It's equivalent to "I am [age] years old"

It is the direct and complete answer to the question `¿Cuántos años tienes?` ("How old are you?").

The verb `tener` ("to have") is used in this phrase because in Spanish age is expressed as something one possesses.
