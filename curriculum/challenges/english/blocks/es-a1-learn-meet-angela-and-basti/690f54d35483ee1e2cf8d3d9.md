# --explanation--

The phrase `Mucho gusto` means "Nice to meet you". 

It is a polite and common way to respond after someone introduces themselves.

This entire phrase is gender-neutral and can be used by any speaker, so you can use it when you're introducing yourself to someone in any situation. For example:

`Mucho gusto. Mi nombre es Noris.` - Nice to meet you. My name is Noris.
