# --description--

Both `¿Y tú?` and `¿Y usted?` mean "And you?"

They are used to quickly return a question to the speaker after you've answered it. This avoids repeating the entire question.

The choice depends on how formal your answer should be. `¿Y tú?` is informal while `¿Y usted?` is formal.
