## Basic Structure

- 0 to 9 have unique words.

- 10 is `十 (shí)`.

- 11 to 19 use `十 (shí)` + the unit digit. For example, 16 is `十六 (shí liù)`.

- Exact tens use the tens digit + `十 (shí)`. For example, 20 is `二十 (èr shí)`.

- Other numbers from 20 to 99 use tens digit + `十 (shí)` + unit digit. For example, 56 is `五十六 (wǔ shí liù)`.
