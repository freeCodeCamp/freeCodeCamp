# --explanation--

`我叫 (wǒ jiào)` means "I'm called...". `我 (wǒ)` means "I", and `叫 (jiào)` means "to be called". It's used when telling someone your name. For example:

`我叫刘明。(wǒ jiào Liu Ming.)` – I'm called Liu Ming. Or, my name is Liu Ming.
