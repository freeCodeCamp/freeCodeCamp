## --text--

What is the half angle formula for `cos(θ/2)`?

## --answers--

`cos(θ/2) = (1 + cos(θ)) / 2`

---

`cos(θ/2) = ±√((1 + cos(θ)) / 2)`

---

`cos(θ/2) = ±√((1 - cos(θ)) / 2)`

---

`cos(θ/2) = √(1 - cos²(θ))`
