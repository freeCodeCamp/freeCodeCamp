## --text--

What is the double angle formula for `sin(2θ)`?

## --answers--

`sin(2θ) = 2 cos²(θ)`

---

`sin(2θ) = cos(2θ)`

---

`sin(2θ) = 2sin(θ)cos(θ)`

---

`sin(2θ) = sin²(θ) - cos²(θ)`
