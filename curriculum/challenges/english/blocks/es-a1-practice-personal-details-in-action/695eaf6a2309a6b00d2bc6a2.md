# --explanation--

The question `¿Cuál es tu nombre y apellido completo?` means "Which is your name and full last name?, another words, "What is your full name?"

`Cuál` - which

`Nombre` - name

`Apellido completo` - full last name(s)

Remember that in Spanish-speaking countries, people typically have **two last names**.
