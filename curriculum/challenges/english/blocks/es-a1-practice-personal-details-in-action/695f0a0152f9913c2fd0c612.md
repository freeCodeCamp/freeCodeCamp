# --description--

This task, is about a clarifying question being asked in Spanish.

**As a reminder**, in Spanish, questions starting with `Cómo`, which means "how", are commonly used like, `¿Cómo te llamas?` (What’s your name?). 

Spanish usually omits the subject pronoun `tú` because the verb form already tells us who the subject is.
