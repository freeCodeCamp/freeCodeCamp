# --explanation--

`Soy uruguaya` means “I am Uruguayan.”

The word `uruguaya` is the feminine form for someone from **Uruguay**. 

Spanish uses gendered nationality terms, so since Julieta is female, she uses `uruguaya` (not `uruguayo`).
