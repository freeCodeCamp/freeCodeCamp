# --explanation--

The question `¿Cuántos años tienes?` means “How old are you?”  

The letter **`ñ`** is not just an "n" with a tilde (˜), it’s a completely separate letter in the Spanish alphabet, and it changes both the pronunciation and meaning of a word.
