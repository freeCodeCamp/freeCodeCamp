## --blanks--

`Cómo`

### --feedback--

This question means “What’s your name?”. Don't forget the accent on the `o`.

---

`Cuál`

### --feedback--

This word is used when asking for specific information, like someone’s last name. Don't forget the accent on the `a`.

---

`Puedes`

### --feedback--

This is the polite way to ask “Can you repeat it?”.

# --explanation--

`¿Cómo te llamas?` – What’s your name?

`Cómo` - how (used idiomatically here)

`¿Cuál es tu apellido?` – What is your last name?

`Cuál` - which

`¿Puedes repetirlo?` – Can you repeat it?

`Puedes` - you can (from the verb `poder`, to be able to/can)
