### --feedback--

This character means "to be called" or "to be named".

# --explanation--

`叫 (jiào)` means "to be called". It's often used after a subject to introduce a name. For example:

`我叫王华 (wǒ jiào Wang Hua)。` – I am called Wang Hua.
