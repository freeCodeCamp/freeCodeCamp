# --explanation--

`我叫 (wǒ jiào...)` means "I am called..." or "My name is...". `叫 (jiào)` means "to be called". Here It's used to show identity. For example:

`我叫王华 (wǒ jiào wáng huá)` — I am called Wang Hua, or, My name is Wang Hua.
