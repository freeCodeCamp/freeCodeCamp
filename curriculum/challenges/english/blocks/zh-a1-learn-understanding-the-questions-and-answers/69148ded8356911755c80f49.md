# --explanation--

`我也是 (wǒ yě shì)` means "me too" or "I am also...". It's a short way to show mutual feelings or agreement in conversations.

For example, when someone says `我很高兴 (wǒ hěn gāo xìng)` meaning "I am very happy", responding with `我也是 (wǒ yě shì)` indicates that you also feel happy.
