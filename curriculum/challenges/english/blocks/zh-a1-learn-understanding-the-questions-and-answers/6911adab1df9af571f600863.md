# --description--

When you want to ask a question expecting a "yes" or "no" answer, you can add the particle `吗 (ma)` to the end of a declarative sentence.

Note that `吗 (ma)` has a neutral tone.

# --explanation--

`吗 (ma)` indicates an interrogative mood. It's used at the end of a declarative sentence to change a statement into a question expecting a "yes" or "no" answer. For example:

`你是开发者吗？(nǐ shì kāi fā zhě ma)` – Are you a developer?
