# --explanation--

`请问 (qǐng wèn)` means "excuse me". It's often used at the start of a question to sound polite. For example:

`请问你是刘明吗 (qǐng wèn nǐ shì Liu Ming ma)？` – Excuse me, are you Liu Ming?
