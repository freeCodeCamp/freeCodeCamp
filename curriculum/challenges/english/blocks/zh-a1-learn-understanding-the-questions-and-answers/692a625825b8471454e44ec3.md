## --text--

Which option means "I'm not"?

## --answers--

`我是 (wǒ shì)`

### --feedback--

This means "I'm", not "I'm not".

---

`不是我 (bù shì wǒ)`

### --feedback--

This means "not me", which is a different structure.

---

`你不是 (nǐ bù shì)`

### --feedback--

This means "you're not".

---

`我不是 (wǒ bù shì)`

## --video-solution--

4

# --explanation--

`我 (wǒ)` means "I". `不是 (bù shì)` means "to be not". Together, `我不是 (wǒ bù shì)` means "I'm not".
