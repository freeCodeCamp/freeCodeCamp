## --text--

Which option shows the correct pair for "China" and "Chinese" as a nationality?

## --answers--

`中国` and `人`

### --feedback--

`人` means "person", not a nationality.

---

`中国` and `中国人`

---

`中国人` and `中文`

### --feedback--

`中文` means "Chinese" as a language, not a nationality.

---

`中国` and `国中人`

### --feedback--

`国中人` is not a correct term in Chinese.

## --video-solution--

2

# --explanation--

`中国 (zhōng guó)` means "China". When it's followed by `人 (rén)`, it forms `中国人 (zhōng guó rén)`, which means "Chinese" as a nationality.
