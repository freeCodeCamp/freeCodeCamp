## --text--

Which option shows the correct pair for "China" and "Chinese" as a nationality?

## --answers--

`中国 (zhōng guó)` and `人 (rén)`

### --feedback--

`人 (rén)` means "person", not a nationality.

---

`中国 (zhōng guó)` and `中国人 (zhōng guó rén)`

---

`中国人 (zhōng guó rén)` and `中文 (zhōng wén)`

### --feedback--

`中文 (zhōng wén)` means "Chinese" as a language, not a nationality.

---

`中国 (zhōng guó)` and `国中人 (guó zhōng rén)`

### --feedback--

`国中人 (guó zhōng rén)` does not mean "Chinese" as a nationality.

# --explanation--

`中国 (zhōng guó)` means "China". When it's followed by `人 (rén)`, it forms `中国人 (zhōng guó rén)`, which means "Chinese" as a nationality.
