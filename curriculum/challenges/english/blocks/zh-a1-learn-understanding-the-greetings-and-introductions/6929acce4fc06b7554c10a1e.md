Which option means "I'm Chinese. I'm a developer."?

## --answers--

# --explanation--

`我是中国人 (wǒ shì zhōng guó rén)` means "I am Chinese." `我是开发者 (wǒ shì kāi fā zhě)` means "I am a developer." These are introductions of nationality and profession.
