# --explanation--

`我是设计师 (wǒ shì shè jì shī)` uses the structure `我是 (wǒ shì...)` + profession to introduce one's job.

`我是 (wǒ shì...)` means "I am…", and `设计师 (shè jì shī)` means "designer". So Liu Ming is saying he is a designer.
