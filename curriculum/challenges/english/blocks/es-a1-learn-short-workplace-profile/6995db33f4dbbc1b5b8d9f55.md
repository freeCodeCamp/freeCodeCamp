# --explanation-- 

The verb `trabajar` means "to work". 

`Trabaja` is the third-person singular form of this verb.

Sebastián asks `¿Dónde trabaja?`, which means "Where does he work?". This is the standard way to ask where someone is currently working in Spanish.
