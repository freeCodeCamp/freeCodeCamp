# --description--

Now let's practice writing Luis' nationality.

# --instructions--

Listen to the audio and complete the sentence below.

# --fillInTheBlank--

## --sentence--

`Es BLANK y BLANK en la oficina de Medellín.`

## --blanks--

`argentino`

### --feedback--

This word describes someone who is from Argentina.

---

`trabaja`

### --feedback--

This is a conjugated form of the verb `trabajar` ("to work").

# --explanation--

`Es argentino` means "He is Argentine". You should know that nationalities in Spanish are only capitalized when they start a sentence.

`Trabaja en la oficina` means "He works in the office". The verb `trabajar` ("to work") is followed by the preposition `en` ("in") to say where he works.

`Medellín` is a very important city in Colombia.
