# --description--

In Spanish, we use the preposition `en` to refer to where something happens or is located.

In a professional context, this includes the company or place where someone works. For example:

- `Trabajo en una oficina.` - I work in an office.

- `Trabajo en Andes Commerce.` - I work at Andes Commerce.
