# --explanation--

`Trabajar en` means "to work in" or "to work at". The preposition `en` is used to indicate the department or company where someone works.

`Aprendizaje` means "learning", `y` means "and", and `desarrollo` means "development".

Therefore, when referring to a male, `trabaja en aprendizaje y desarrollo` means "he works in learning and development".
