# --description--

In Spanish, we use `su` ("his"/"her") to talk about someone else's email address.

An email address also has a few key symbols that you should be familiar with:

- `Correo` - Email.

- `@` - `Arroba` - At.

- `.` - `Punto` - Dot/period.

`Correo electr√≥nico` is the formal and complete way of referring to an email, but depending on the context, it's usually okay to just say `correo`.
