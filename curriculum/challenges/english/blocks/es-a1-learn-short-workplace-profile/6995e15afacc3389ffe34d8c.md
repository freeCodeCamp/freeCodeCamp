# --explanation--

`¿Cuál es...?` means "What is...?" Remember to include the accent mark on the letter `a`.

In this context, `su` means "his" or "her".

`Correo electrónico` means "email". In casual conversation, it's very common to abbreviate it to `correo`. The complete version is commonly used in professional contexts. 

The word `correo` means "mail" and the word `electrónico` means "electronic". Remember to include the accent mark in the first letter `o` of `electrónico`.
