# --explanation--

The sentence `No, es de México` translates to "No, she is from Mexico".

In Spanish, we use the word `no` to negate something. For example:

`No, él no es doctor.` - No, he is not a doctor.

Therefore, Sebastián is saying that Marisol is not from Colombia, she is from Mexico.

In Spanish, the country `México` must carry an accent mark on the letter `e`.
