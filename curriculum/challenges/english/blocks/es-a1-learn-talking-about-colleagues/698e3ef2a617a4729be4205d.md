# --explanation--

Mateo asks `¿Dónde vive Valeria?`, which means "Where does Valeria live?"

`¿Dónde...?` means "Where...?" 

Remember to include an opening question mark `¿` and to include the accent mark in the letter `ó` when this word starts a question.

The word `vive` means "lives", the third-person singular of the verb `vivir`.
