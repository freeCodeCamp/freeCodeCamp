# --description--

When you're talking about someone else, you can use this question to ask about their name:

`¿Cómo se llama?` - What is his/her name?

The word `cómo` means "how". It's very important to write the accent mark on the letter `o` when this word starts a question.

The word `se` implies that you're talking about a third person.

Elena is talking to Esteban about his new colleague, Diego.
