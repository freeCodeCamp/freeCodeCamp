# --explanation--

Camila asks `¿Cuál es su profesión?`, which means "What is her profession?"

In this question, the word `su` indicates that Camila is talking about a third person. 

Remember to start the question with an opening question mark `¿`, and to include the accent mark in the words `Cuál` and `profesión`. This is very important for writing these words correctly.
