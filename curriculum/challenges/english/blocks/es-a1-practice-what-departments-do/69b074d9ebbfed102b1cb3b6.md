# --explanation--

The correct answer is `El departamento de marketing` because the audio says `El departamento de marketing promueve la empresa.` 

Here, `promueve` shows the action of promoting, and `la empresa` refers to the company. 

`Promueve` comes from `promover` (to promote), an `-er` verb with a stem change (`o` becomes `ue` in the stressed syllable). This is why the form looks different from the `-ar` verbs you practiced earlier:

`-ar` verbs: `desarrolla`, `verifica`, `analiza`, `apoya`, `capacita`, `diseña`

`-er` verb with stem change: `promueve`

Despite the different ending, the principle is the same: one singular department, one 3rd person singular verb.
