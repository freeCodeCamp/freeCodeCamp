# --description--

In this task, you will practice the third-person singular conjugation of the verb `crear` for a singular subject.

# --instructions--

Choose the correct sentence.

# --questions--

## --text--

Which of the following sentences has the correct conjugation of the verb `crear`?

## --answers--

`La empresa crear productos digitales.`

### --feedback--

`Crear` is the infinitive form of the verb. You should use a different form of this verb to match the subject.

---

`La empresa crean productos digitales.`

### --feedback--

`Crean` is the third-person **plural** form. The subject `la empresa` is singular.

---

`La empresa creo productos digitales.`

### --feedback--

`Creo` is the first-person singular form. The subject here is `la empresa`, not `yo`.

---

`La empresa crea productos digitales.`

## --video-solution--

4

# --explanation--

`La empresa crea productos digitales.` means "the company creates digital products".

The verb must agree with its subject. `La empresa` is singular, so `crear` conjugates as `crea` in third person.

These are verb conjugations for different subjects:

- `Crear` — infinitive (never used after a subject on its own).
- `Yo creo` — I create.
- `Tú creas` — You create (informal).
- `Él/ella/la empresa crea` — He/She/The company creates.
- `Ellos/ellas crean` — They create.

This also applies to other verbs like `trabaja` and `ayuda`, and many other regular `-ar` verbs.
