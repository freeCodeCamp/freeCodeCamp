# --description--

When someone makes a correct assumption or guess about you, you can confirm it using phrases like `that's right`. It's a way of agreeing or saying yes to what is said.

# --questions--

## --text--

## --answers--

### --feedback--

`That's wrong` is used to disagree.

---

`that's okay`

### --feedback--

---
