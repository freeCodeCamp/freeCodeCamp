# --explanation--

The greeting `Hola, buenas noches` means "Hello, good evening" or "Hello, good night".

`Buenas` is the feminine plural form of "good".

`Noches` means "nights".

Together, they form the standard Spanish greeting for the evening: **"Good evening"** or **"Good night."**
