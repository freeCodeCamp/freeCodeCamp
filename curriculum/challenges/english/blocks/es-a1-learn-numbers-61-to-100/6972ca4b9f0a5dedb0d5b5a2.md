These are the numbers in the blanks:

- 90 - `Noventa`.

- 92 - `Noventa y dos`.

- 96 - `Noventa y seis`.

- 99 - `Noventa y nueve`.

Remember that numbers from 91 to 99 follow this structure: 

`Noventa` (90) + `y` ("and") + digit.
