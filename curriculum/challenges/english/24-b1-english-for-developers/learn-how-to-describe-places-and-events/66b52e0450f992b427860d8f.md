# --description--

`How often` is used to ask about the frequency of an event. For example:

- `How often do you exercise?` - You are asking about the number of times someone exercises in a given period.
- `How often does the bus come?` - You are asking about the frequency of the bus service.

`To hold` can mean to organize or conduct an event. It is often used when talking about meetings, classes, or events. For example:

- `We hold meetings every Monday.` - We organize meetings every Monday.
- `They hold an annual conference.` - They organize a conference once a year.
