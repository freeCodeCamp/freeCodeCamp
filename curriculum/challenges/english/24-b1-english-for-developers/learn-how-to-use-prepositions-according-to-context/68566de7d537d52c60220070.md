# --explanation--

`Until` means something will continue to a certain point in time. For example:

`He will stay until Monday.` – This means he will leave on or after Monday.

`By the end of` is used to describe something happening before or close to the final part of a time period. For example:

`We expect results by the end of the week.` – This means results should come before the week finishes.
