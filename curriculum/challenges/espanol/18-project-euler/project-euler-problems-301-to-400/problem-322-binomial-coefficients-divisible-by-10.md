# --description--

Let $T(m, n)$ be the number of the binomial coefficients ${}^iC_n$ that are divisible by 10 for $n ≤ i &lt; m$ ($i$, $m$ and $n$ are positive integers).

Está dado que $T({10}^9, {10}^7 - 10) = 989\\,697\\,000$.

Encontrar $T({10}^{18}, {10}^{12} - 10)$.
