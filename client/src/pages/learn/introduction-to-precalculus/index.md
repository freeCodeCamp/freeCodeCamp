## Introduction to Precalculus

Precalculus is a branch of mathematics that prepares students for the study of calculus. It combines algebra, geometry, and trigonometry to provide a solid foundation for understanding more advanced mathematical concepts.

In this comprehensive course, you will learn about functions, equations, and inequalities, as well as trigonometric identities and formulas.

You will also explore advanced topics such as conic sections, polar coordinates, and parametric equations.
